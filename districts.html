Districts

Kerala's 14 districts are distributed among six regions: North Malabar (far-north Kerala), South Malabar (northern Kerala), Kochi (central Kerala), Northern Travancore, Central Travancore (southern Kerala) and Southern Travancore (far-south Kerala). These 14 districts are each famous for its own set of unique destinations and features. Following is a list of that can be used to gain information on each individual district based on your preference. This is to aid you in the process of gaining more data regarding the areas you would like to visit and plan your trip accordingly.
